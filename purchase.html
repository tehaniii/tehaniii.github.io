SAVE THE SEA TURTLES

Type of donations

Donations are essential for the operation of turtle hatcheries in Sri Lanka. These hatcheries work hard to protect endangered sea turtles, but they require financial assistance to meet their costs. Donations assist with things like facility upkeep, turtle health care, research, and education. They also assist with the purchase of essential goods such as nesting materials and turtle food. This is why your donation immediately supports the operation of these hatcheries and the protection of sea turtles in Sri Lanka's coastal areas. There are numerous forms of donations that visitors may offer to help turtle hatcheries in Sri Lanka. Here are some examples of popular forms of donations:

Funding

Making financial contributions to organizations working towards turtle conservation can help fund for conservation projects, rescue and rehabilitation efforts as well as research projects.

Adoption

Another way to help is by symbolically adopting a turtle through adoption programs offered by organizations.tourists can provide financial support for the protection of these turtles in return for updates on the adopted turtles progress and other benefits.

Volunteer

Tourists can also choose to volunteer at sea turtle conservation projects in Sri Lanka.These programs allow hands-on activities such as beach patrols,nest mnitering,hatchery management and public awareness campaigns

Learn more about volunteer work in Sri Lanka

Beach Cleanups

Tourists can support or participate in beach cleanups that are organized by schools, universities and other societies.These cleanups initiatives help keep turtle nesting beaches free from any litter and pollution, allowing for a safer environment for these beautiful creatures

Merchandise

Tourists and visitors can contribute by buying merchandise that diretly contribute to sea turtle conservation efforts.Buying local merchandise in Sri Lanka not only allows you to carry home one-of-a-kind souvenirs, but it also directly benefits the lives of sea turtles. By purchasing these goods, you are supporting sea turtle hatcheries' conservation efforts and helping to safeguard these amazing creatures. Here are some examples of locally produced goods:

Handmade Turtle Crafts

Support local artisans by purchasing handcrafted turtle-themed crafts like pottery and sculptures, which showcase their talent while supporting sea turtle hatcheries.

Turtle-themed Souvenirs

Choose from a range of turtle-themed souvenirs, such as t-shirts, keychains, and postcards, which promote awareness and support for sea turtle conservation.Also Explore a wide selection of beautifully handcrafted jewelry pieces made from sustainable materials, featuring sea turtle motifs.

Eco friendly products

Consider purchasing environmentally friendly things such as reusable bags, water bottles, and bamboo products. These purchases contribute to the reduction of plastic trash, which endangers marine turtles and their habitats.

About

The Sri Lanka Turtle Hatchery is a major sanctuary that contributes significantly to the preservation and maintenance of the diversified sea turtle population in the nation. The hatchery tirelessly protects sea turtle nests against predation and theft thanks to its enthusiastic and committed employees and volunteers, guaranteeing the survival of these amazing animals. The hatchery cares for the eggs and monitors hatching, ensuring that the young are released safely into the ocean and helping to protect these magnificent sea turtles and the sensitive marine habitats they call home.
